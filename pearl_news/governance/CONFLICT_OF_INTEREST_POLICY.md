## Purpose

Pearl News avoids conflicts of interest that could compromise our independence or the trust of our audience. We disclose relevant affiliations and manage conflicts in coverage.

## Disclosure requirements

- **Staff, contributors, and board:** Anyone with a direct relationship to an organization or person we cover must disclose that relationship to editors.
- **Forum and teachers:** When we quote or feature participants in the United Spiritual Leaders Forum (or affiliated teachers), we may transparently note that relationship where relevant (e.g. “X, who participates in the United Spiritual Leaders Forum hosted by Pearl News…”). We do not allow that relationship to dictate coverage or to create promotional content.
- **Funders and partners:** We disclose funding sources and partnerships as required by our governance and editorial standards.

## How we manage conflicts

- We do not let affiliations determine story selection or framing in a way that promotes a single interest.
- We require at least one non-forum source or voice per article to ensure balance.
- When a conflict is unavoidable (e.g. covering an event we host), we disclose it clearly and maintain journalistic standards in the piece.

## Accountability

Governance and editorial leadership are responsible for enforcing this policy. Violations are addressed through internal review and, where appropriate, correction or retraction.
