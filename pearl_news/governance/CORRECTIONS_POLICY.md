## Our commitment

Pearl News corrects factual errors promptly and transparently. We do not alter articles in a way that obscures what was wrong or when a correction was made.

## How we correct

- **Substantive factual errors:** We update the article with a clear correction notice (e.g. at the top or bottom of the article, or inline where appropriate). The notice states what was corrected and when.
- **Minor errors (typos, style):** We may correct without a separate notice; we do not change meaning or facts silently.
- **Retractions:** If an article is fundamentally flawed or misleading, we may retract it and replace with a short explanation and link to a corrected version if one is published.

## How to report an error

Contact: [Insert editorial contact email]. We review all submissions and respond to substantive correction requests.

## Accountability

Our editorial standards and governance require that we maintain integrity in corrections. Corrections are noted in our records and, where applicable, in the build manifest or audit trail for the article.
