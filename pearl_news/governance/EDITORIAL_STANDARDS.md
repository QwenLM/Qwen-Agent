## Commitment

Pearl News is committed to accurate, balanced, transparent, and independent civic journalism. We report on global events and explore how spiritual and ethical traditions contribute constructively to challenges facing youth, within the framework of the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

## Core standards

1. **Editorial independence** — No external control of individual articles; no promotional function for any teacher or organization.
2. **Accuracy and verification** — Every article cites reputable sources; we distinguish fact from commentary and correct errors promptly.
3. **News vs commentary** — News and analysis are clearly separated from opinion; commentary is explicitly labeled.
4. **Youth lens** — Youth impact is specific (data, age group, or region where applicable), not generic.
5. **SDG/UN references** — We reference SDGs and UN bodies accurately and do not imply endorsement or partnership unless formally documented.
6. **Transparency and disclosure** — We disclose affiliations when relevant and maintain a conflict-of-interest policy.

## Sourcing

- News templates require at least two external sources.
- Every article includes at least one non-forum source or voice (e.g. UN agency, external expert, or reputable news outlet).

## Corrections

See our [Corrections policy](CORRECTIONS_POLICY.md). Errors are corrected promptly and transparently.
