Pearl News is an incorporated nonprofit civic media organization. We report on global events and examine how spiritual and ethical leaders contribute to youth resilience in alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

## Editorial independence

Editorial decisions are made by the Pearl News editorial team. Spiritual leaders, forum participants, partner organizations, and board members do not approve or control individual articles. Pearl News does not function as promotional media for any religious teacher or organization.

## Relationship to the United Nations

Pearl News supports and references the UN Sustainable Development Goals as a global framework. We do **not** claim official UN partnership, UN endorsement, or authorization to represent UN bodies. Any reference to UN agencies or initiatives is for journalistic and educational purposes only.

**United Nations Spiritual Alliance For Youth** is a separately incorporated nonprofit and operates with its own governance. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to the United Nations unless explicitly stated in formal public documentation.

## Transparency

We disclose relevant affiliations when covering organizations connected to our initiatives. Governance and editorial standards are published and updated as needed.
